Malabar es la joya de Cerritos: conjuntos cerrados de alta seguridad, lotes amplios, vegetación exuberante y vistas privilegiadas al lago. For International Buyers

Why Colombia.
Why Now.

Colombia's Eje Cafetero is one of Latin America's most compelling luxury real estate markets. Strong fundamentals, UNESCO-recognized natural beauty, and favorable exchange rates make this an exceptional window for international investors and diaspora buyers.

Investor Visa Eligibility

Colombia's Visa M (Investor category) allows foreign nationals who invest a minimum of 650 SMMLV (~USD $170,000) in real estate to obtain a renewable Colombian visa — a clear path to residency in one of South America's most vibrant economies.

UNESCO Coffee Region

The Coffee Cultural Landscape of Colombia is a UNESCO World Heritage Site surrounding Pereira. It draws a growing international audience of experiential travelers and investors seeking authentic cultural destinations with world-class connectivity.

USD Exchange Advantage

With the COP/USD exchange currently favorable for dollar-holders, at ~$4,100 COP per USD this property represents exceptional value compared to equivalent luxury real estate in Miami, Medellín's El Poblado, or Panama City — with 12%+ annual appreciation.
